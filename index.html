Prologue


Do you love to immerse yourself in imaginary worlds, create memorable characters, and experience thrilling adventures? If so, you might be interested in joining our pen and paper roleplaying club! We are a group of enthusiasts who meet regularly to play tabletop role-playing games (TRPGs).

TRPGs are games in which you and your friends take on the roles of fictional characters and collaborate to tell a story. You can choose from a variety of genres, such as fantasy, science fiction, horror, or historical, and use dice and rules to determine the outcomes of your actions.

If you are interested, please use our contact form down below for more information. We hope to see you soon at our next session!


The Town Crier


Welcome to the news section of our pen and paper roleplaying club! Here you can find the latest updates on our upcoming events and workshops, as well as other announcements and information.


Events


We have some exciting events planned for the next few months, and we hope you can join us for some fun and adventure!


  • April 10, 2023: We are hosting a one-shot session of Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition at "The George & Dragon". A one-shot is a standalone adventure that can be completed in one session, without any prior commitment or character creation. It’s a great way to try out a new game or system, or to introduce someone to the hobby. The session will start at 6 pm and last for about 3 hours. We have space for 8 players, and the game will be run by our experienced dungeon master, Alex. If you are interested, please sign up here by April 8, 2023.

  • May 5, 2023: We are organizing a game night at "The George & Dragon". This is a casual and social event where you can meet other members of the club, play some board games or card games, or just chat and have a drink. The game night will start at 8 pm and end at 11 pm. There is no need to register, just show up and have fun!

  • June 17, 2023: We are participating in the Free RPG Day at "Warhammer". Free RPG Day is an annual event where game publishers and retailers offer free samples of their products, such as rulebooks, modules, dice, or miniatures. It’s a great opportunity to discover new games, get some freebies, and support your local game store. We will be there from 10 am to 6 pm, and we will be running some demo sessions of various TRPGs, such as Call of Cthulhu, Cyberpunk Red, Star Wars, and more. If you want to join us, please check the schedule here and sign up for the sessions you want to play.

Workshops


We also have some workshops planned for the next few months, where you can learn new skills or improve your existing ones. These workshops are open to anyone who wants to learn more about TRPGs, whether you are a beginner or an expert.


  • April 24, 2023: We are offering a workshop on character creation at "The George & Dragon". Character creation is one of the most important and enjoyable aspects of TRPGs, as it allows you to express your creativity and personality through your character. In this workshop, you will learn how to create a character for any TRPG system, using some basic steps and guidelines. You will also learn how to write a character background, choose a character class and race, assign attributes and skills, and select equipment and abilities. The workshop will start at 2 pm and last for about 2 hours. We have space for 12 participants, and the workshop will be led by our experienced player, Lisa. If you are interested, please sign up here by April 22, 2023.

  • May 19, 2023: We are offering a workshop on game mastering at "Warhammer". Game mastering is one of the most challenging and rewarding roles in TRPGs, as it involves creating and running the game world, the story, and the non-player characters. In this workshop, you will learn how to be a good game master for any TRPG system, using some tips and tricks. You will also learn how to prepare and improvise a game session, design and balance encounters, create and portray NPCs, and handle different situations and player styles. The workshop will start at 2 pm and last for about 3 hours. We have space for 10 participants, and the workshop will be led by our experienced game master, Alex. If you are interested, please sign up here by May 17, 2023.
